Ferrari Team

Carlos and Charles

In the scarlet embrace of the Prancing Horse, Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz, two distinct spirits, dance on the razor's edge of speed and grace. Charles, the Monegasque virtuoso, weaves through the labyrinth of tarmac with a youthful exuberance, leaving the echoes of victories and podiums in his wake. His driving is a symphony, a fusion of precision and daring, as he etches his name into the storied annals of Ferrari's racing legacy.

Beside him, the Spanish maestro, Carlos Sainz, lends his seasoned artistry to the ballet of speed. With the wisdom of circuits traveled and battles waged, Sainz brings the cadence of experience to the team. Each lap is a brushstroke on the canvas of their shared destiny, a fusion of passion and technique that embodies the spirit of Ferrari.

Together, Leclerc and Sainz carve their saga, painting the asphalt with the hues of ambition and camaraderie, as Ferrari's timeless allure finds resonance in the harmony of their racing partnership.

Who are current drivers?

Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz, the Spanish racing virtuoso, epitomizes the convergence of passion and precision in the high-octane world of Formula 1. Born into a family deeply entrenched in motorsport, Sainz carries a legacy that resonates with his relentless pursuit of excellence on the racetrack. With a career spanning various teams, Carlos has demonstrated a rare blend of consistency and flair, earning him a reputation as a seasoned driver capable of extracting the utmost from his machinery.

In 2021, Sainz embarked on a new chapter as he joined the prestigious Scuderia Ferrari, bringing with him not just speed, but the sagacity of a seasoned campaigner. His partnership with Ferrari signifies more than a collaboration between man and machine.

Charles Leclerc

In the vibrant tapestry of Formula 1, Charles Leclerc emerges as a young maestro, his driving prowess a symphony of precision and audacity. Hailing from the motorsport-rich enclave of Monaco, Leclerc's journey to the upper echelons of racing mirrors the rise of a prodigy destined for greatness. From his early days in the Ferrari Driver Academy to leading the charge for Scuderia Ferrari, Leclerc's ascent is marked by a fearless approach to each corner and an uncanny ability to transform pressure into performance.

The Monegasque sensation, with his cherubic charm off the track, belies the ferocity he unleashes within the cockpit. His debut season with Ferrari in 2019 saw him defy expectations, claiming victories and podiums with an artistry that transcends his youth.
